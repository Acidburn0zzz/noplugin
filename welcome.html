Getting Started

NoPlugin tries to play QuickTime, RealPlayer, and Windows Media content using your browser's built-in HTML5 player. If your browser cannot play the content, NoPlugin will play it with the media player on your computer.

If you don't have it already, you can download VLC Media Player to play plugin content that cannot be played in the browser.
